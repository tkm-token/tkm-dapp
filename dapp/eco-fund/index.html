Dynamic Adjustment Valve & Weekly Incentive Program

To recognize members who have been actively promoting and making outstanding contributions at the forefront of the market, the TKM Joint Construction Ecosystem has officially introduced the "Dynamic Adjustment Valve" mechanism and integrated it into the daily burn distribution incentive program. Rewards under this program will be distributed weekly, with specific rules as follows:

1. Weekly Promotion Ranking Rewards

  • Starting from 00:00 every Monday, all new promotion performance from validators will be automatically recognized by the smart contract and fairly ranked across the entire network.
  • At the settlement time of 24:00 every Sunday, the top 99 validators will receive generous rewards based on their rankings.

2. Community Service Allowance Standards

  • During the statistical period, if the total new promotion performance from your direct referrals (after deducting the highest-performing line) reaches 25,000 USDT, you will receive a $500 service allowance.
  • Allowances can be accumulated progressively, with a monthly cap of $2,500.
  • Performance already counted towards allowances will not be reused in statistics. After monthly allowances are distributed, related performance will be reset to zero and accumulation will restart the following month.
